Day-wise Itinerary

Detailed plan for your monsoon adventure

Day 0

Departure from Ahmedabad

  • Board night train from Ahmedabad to Mumbai (recommended trains: Gujarat Mail 12902 at 22:50 or BVC BDTS SF EXP 12972 at 23:35)
  • Sleep in train (7-8 hours journey)

Pro Tips:

  • Book sleeper or 3AC for comfort
  • Carry snacks and water
  • Download entertainment for the journey
Estimated Cost: Train fare as per chosen class
Day 1

Mumbai to Matheran

  • Arrive Mumbai early morning (6:15-8:15 AM)
  • Take local train from Mumbai Central/Dadar to Neral (1.5-2 hours)
  • From Neral: Toy train or shared taxi to Matheran
  • Check-in to accommodation
  • Horse riding exploration (Panorama Point, Echo Point, Charlotte Lake)
  • Evening sunset at Sunset Point

Pro Tips:

  • Carry raincoats in July
  • Book horses in advance
  • Entry fee ‚Çπ50 per person
Estimated Cost: Local train + toy train/taxi + accommodation + horse riding + entry fee
Day 2

Matheran Walking Tour & Travel to Imagica

  • Morning walking tour (Louisa Point, King George Point, One Tree Hill)
  • Visit local markets
  • Afternoon: Travel to Imagica (1.5-2 hours by taxi)
  • Check-in near Imagica
  • Evening: Relax or explore Imagica vicinity

Pro Tips:

  • Start early for walking tour
  • Pack light for travel
  • Book Imagica tickets online
Estimated Cost: Taxi to Imagica + accommodation + meals
Day 3

Imagica & Return to Ahmedabad

  • Full day at Imagica Theme Park
  • Enjoy rides, shows, and attractions
  • Evening: Travel to Mumbai
  • Night train back to Ahmedabad

Pro Tips:

  • Use express tickets for popular rides
  • Carry water bottle
  • Book return train in advance
Estimated Cost: Imagica tickets + meals + taxi to Mumbai + return train
